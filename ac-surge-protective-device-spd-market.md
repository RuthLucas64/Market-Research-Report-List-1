<p><p>The AC Surge Protective Device (SPD) market is primarily divided into two types: solid-state AC SPDs and hybrid AC SPDs. Solid-state AC SPDs utilize semiconductor technology for rapid response to voltage surges, offering high reliability and efficiency. In contrast, hybrid AC SPDs combine both solid-state elements and traditional components, providing enhanced protection and voltage clamping capability. These devices are essential for safeguarding electrical equipment from transient overvoltages, ensuring system longevity and operational continuity in various applications.</p></p>
