<p><p>The Adhesiveless Flexible Copper Clad Laminate (FCCL) market is segmented into two main types: single-sided non-adhesive FCCL and double-sided non-adhesive FCCL. Single-sided non-adhesive FCCL features a copper layer on one side, ideal for applications requiring simple circuitry and flexibility. In contrast, double-sided non-adhesive FCCL consists of copper layers on both sides, providing enhanced connectivity and circuit density. Both types are utilized in various electronic devices, offering benefits like improved thermal performance and reduced weight without the need for adhesives.</p></p>
