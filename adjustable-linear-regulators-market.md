<p><p>The adjustable linear regulators market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel regulators provide a stable voltage output for one load, making them ideal for simpler applications with minimal power needs. Conversely, multi-channel regulators offer several adjustable outputs, catering to more complex designs that require multiple voltage levels. These regulators are increasingly preferred in automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial applications due to their efficiency, simplicity, and reduced component count, adapted to varied system requirements.</p></p>
