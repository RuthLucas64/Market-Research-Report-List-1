<p><p>The air sampling system market is primarily divided into two main types: pipe detectors and smoke detectors. Pipe detectors utilize a network of pipes to draw air samples and identify smoke or fire conditions efficiently, ideal for large and complex spaces. Smoke detectors, on the other hand, are installed as standalone devices that detect smoke particles in the air, providing immediate alerts for fire threats. Both systems are crucial for enhancing safety and early warning in residential, commercial, and industrial environments.</p></p>
