<p><p>The Aluminum Nitride (AlN) filler market comprises various production methods, primarily including the Direct Nitridation Method, Carbothermal Reduction, and Nitridation Method. The Direct Nitridation Method involves directly reacting aluminum with nitrogen, yielding high-purity AlN. The Carbothermal Reduction and Nitridation Method combines aluminum oxide with carbon and nitrogen, producing AlN through a two-step process. Each method impacts the material's purity, particle size, and thermal conductivity, catering to diverse applications in electronics, ceramics, and thermal management solutions.</p></p>
