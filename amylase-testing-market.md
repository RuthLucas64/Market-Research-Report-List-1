<p><p>Amylase testing is utilized to diagnose and monitor conditions related to the pancreas, such as pancreatitis. The market is divided into two main types: blood sample testing and urine sample testing. Blood sample testing measures amylase levels in the bloodstream, providing immediate insights into pancreatic function. In contrast, urine sample testing assesses the amount of amylase excreted in urine over a period, which can reflect long-term pancreatic issues. Both methods aid in effective patient diagnosis and management.</p></p>
