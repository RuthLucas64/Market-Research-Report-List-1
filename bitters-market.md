<p><p>Bitters can be categorized into four main market types. Cocktail bitters are primarily used to enhance the flavor of cocktails, contributing complexity and depth. Aperitif bitters are designed to stimulate appetite and are typically enjoyed before meals. Digestif bitters, consumed after meals, aid digestion and promote relaxation. Each type serves distinct purposes in culinary and therapeutic contexts, appealing to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
