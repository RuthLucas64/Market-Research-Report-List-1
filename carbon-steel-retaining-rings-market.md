<p><strong>The Carbon Steel Retaining Rings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Retaining Rings</li><li>Internal Retaining Rings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon steel retaining rings market is segmented into external and internal retaining rings. External retaining rings fit around shafts or housings, providing a secure grip and preventing components from sliding off. They are commonly used in automotive and machinery applications. In contrast, internal retaining rings fit into grooves in housings or bore holes, holding components in place within a cavity. Both types are vital for assembly stability and reliability, ensuring that parts remain securely positioned under operational stresses.</p></p>
