<p><p>Centrifugal canned motor pumps are categorized by various types to meet specific applications. Standard basic pumps are designed for general fluid transfer. Reverse circulation pumps facilitate fluid movement in the opposite direction, often for cooling or heating applications. High temperature pumps handle fluids at elevated temperatures. Self-priming pumps can create a vacuum to draw fluids without external assistance. Multistage pumps are used for high pressure applications, involving multiple impellers. Other variations may include specialty pumps tailored for unique requirements.</p></p>
