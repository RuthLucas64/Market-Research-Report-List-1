<p><p>Chain couplings are essential components in mechanical transmission systems, facilitating the connection of aligned shafts. Roller chain couplings are designed with interlocking rollers that allow for flexibility and accommodate misalignment, making them ideal for high-torque applications. On the other hand, nylon chain couplings utilize nylon materials, offering a lightweight and corrosion-resistant option. They are suitable for applications requiring lower loads and reduced noise, catering to diverse industries while enhancing efficiency and reliability in power transmission systems.</p></p>
