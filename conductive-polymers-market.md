<p><ul><li>Electrically Conducting Polymers</li><li>Thermally Conducting Polymers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conductive polymers market is categorized into electrically conducting polymers and thermally conducting polymers. Electrically conducting polymers, such as polyaniline and polypyrrole, are utilized in applications like sensors, batteries, and electronic circuits due to their ability to conduct electricity while retaining flexibility. On the other hand, thermally conducting polymers, often used in heat management applications, provide efficient heat dissipation while being lightweight and corrosion-resistant. Both types have crucial roles in advancing technology across various industries, including electronics, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
