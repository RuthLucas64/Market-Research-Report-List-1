<p><p>End Stage Renal Disease (ESRD) therapy encompasses various treatment options to manage advanced kidney failure. Hemodialysis involves filtering blood through a dialyzer, typically performed in a clinic or at home, while peritoneal dialysis uses the abdominal lining to clean the blood via a catheter. Kidney transplantation involves surgically placing a healthy kidney from a donor into a patient. Each therapy type offers unique benefits and challenges, catering to different patient needs and preferences in managing ESRD.</p></p>
