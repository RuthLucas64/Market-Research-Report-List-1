<p><strong>The Fiber-optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber-optic cable market is primarily divided into two types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode cables. Single-Mode cables feature a smaller core diameter, allowing for the transmission of light over long distances with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for telecommunications and long-range applications. In contrast, Multi-Mode cables have a larger core, enabling multiple light paths and making them suitable for shorter distances, such as within data centers and local area networks. Both types serve distinct needs in the communication infrastructure.</p></p>
