<p><p>The Front Illuminated (FI) CMOS sensor market comprises two main types: Passive Pixel Sensor (PPS) and Active Pixel Sensor (APS) CMOS technologies. PPS utilizes a simpler structure with lower noise levels but limited image quality, making it suitable for basic applications. In contrast, APS incorporates an active pixel structure, improving image quality and enabling features like higher resolution and better sensitivity. This distinction drives their use in various consumer electronics, influencing market dynamics and product innovations.</p></p>
