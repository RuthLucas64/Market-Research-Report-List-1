<p><p>FTTx cabinets are essential components in fiber-optic networks, designed to house equipment for fiber-to-the-home (FTTH) or fiber-to-the-building (FTTB) deployments. Single cabin types are compact units that often serve smaller neighborhoods or buildings, accommodating limited fiber connections and equipment.
