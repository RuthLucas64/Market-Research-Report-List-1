<p><p>The high-intensity natural sweetener market is divided into two main types: nutritive sweeteners and non-nutritive sweeteners. Nutritive sweeteners provide calories and are derived from natural sources, such as honey or agave syrup, and are often used for their flavor and energy content. Non-nutritive sweeteners, on the other hand, offer sweetness without significant calories, making them popular for low-calorie and sugar-free products. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking alternatives to traditional sugar.</p></p>
