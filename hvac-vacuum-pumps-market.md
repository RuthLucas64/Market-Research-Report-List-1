<p><p>The HVAC vacuum pumps market is primarily categorized into single-stage and double-stage types. Single-stage pumps are designed for applications requiring lower vacuum levels and are generally more cost-effective and simpler to operate. In contrast, double-stage pumps achieve deeper vacuums quickly and efficiently, making them suitable for more demanding applications in refrigeration and air conditioning systems. Double-stage pumps tend to offer enhanced performance, reducing moisture and contaminants more effectively, which is crucial for maintaining system integrity and efficiency.</p></p>
