<p><ul><li>Laser Mask Writer</li><li>Electron Beam Mask Writer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor market utilizes specialized mask writers for photomasks, essential for chip fabrication. Laser mask writers employ high-speed lasers to create precise patterns on photographic materials, offering efficient and cost-effective solutions for lower-resolution applications. In contrast, electron beam mask writers utilize focused beams of electrons to achieve ultra-high precision, enabling the production of intricate designs required for advanced semiconductor nodes. Both technologies cater to varying demands in the semiconductor industry, balancing speed, resolution, and production scalability.</p></p>
