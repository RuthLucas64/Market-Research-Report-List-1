<p><ul><li>Dual Color</li><li>Multi-color</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microdeletion probes market is categorized into dual-color and multi-color segments. Dual-color probes utilize two distinct fluorescent dyes to highlight specific chromosomal regions, facilitating the identification of deletions or duplications. This method enhances contrast and clarity in analysis. On the other hand, multi-color probes employ three or more fluorescent dyes, allowing for a more comprehensive view of genetic abnormalities. This approach can simultaneously detect multiple chromosomal alterations, providing deeper insights into complex genetic conditions and improving diagnostic accuracy.</p></p>
