<p><p>The mozzarella cheese powder market is divided into two main types: natural cheese powder and processed cheese powder. Natural cheese powder is derived from real mozzarella cheese, offering a pure, authentic flavor, and is often used in gourmet foods, snacks, and culinary applications. Processed cheese powder, on the other hand, is made from cheese products that undergo additional processing for enhanced melting properties and shelf stability. This type is commonly used in convenience foods, instant products, and seasoning mixes.</p></p>
