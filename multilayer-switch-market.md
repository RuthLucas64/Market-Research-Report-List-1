<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switch</li><li>Layer 3 Switch</li><li>Layer 4 Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multilayer switch market comprises Layer 2, Layer 3, and Layer 4 switches, each serving distinct networking functions. Layer 2 switches handle data at the data link layer, managing traffic within local networks. Layer 3 switches incorporate routing capabilities, directing data between different networks. Layer 4 switches add functionality by considering transport layer information, enabling advanced traffic management and prioritization. Together, these switches enhance network efficiency and performance, catering to varying requirements in enterprise and data center environments.</p></p>
