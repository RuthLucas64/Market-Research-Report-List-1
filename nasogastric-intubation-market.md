<p><strong>The Nasogastric Intubation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Levin Catheter</li><li>Salem Sump Catheter</li><li>Dobhoff Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nasogastric intubation market includes three primary types: Levin catheters, Salem sump catheters, and Dobhoff tubes. Levin catheters are simple, single-lumen tubes used for feeding, medication administration, or gastric decompression. Salem sump catheters feature a dual-lumen design, allowing for simultaneous aspiration and venting, often utilized in post-operative care. Dobhoff tubes are softer, smaller diameter tubes primarily designed for enteral feeding, promoting patient comfort and decreasing the risk of irritation. Each type serves distinct clinical needs.</p></p>
