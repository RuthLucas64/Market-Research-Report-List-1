<p><p>Non-concentrating solar collectors are devices that capture solar energy without using lenses or mirrors to focus sunlight. These systems typically utilize flat-plate collectors or evacuated tube collectors to convert solar radiation into thermal energy for applications such as water heating and space heating. Their simplicity, cost-effectiveness, and ease of installation make them popular in both residential and commercial sectors.</p><p>The Non-concentrating Solar Collector Market is witnessing significant growth due to increasing awareness of renewable energy sources, government incentives, and rising energy costs.
