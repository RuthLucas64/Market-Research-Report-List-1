<p><ul><li>Polarization Dependent Opto-Isolators</li><li>Polarization Independent Opto-Isolators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The opto-isolators market is segmented into polarization-dependent and polarization-independent types. Polarization-dependent opto-isolators use the polarization state of light to achieve isolation, making them ideal for specific applications where polarization control is critical. In contrast, polarization-independent opto-isolators offer flexibility by functioning effectively regardless of light polarization, making them suitable for a broader range of uses. Both types are essential in ensuring signal integrity and safety in various electronic and communication systems.</p></p>
