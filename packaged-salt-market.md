<p><p>The packaged salt market comprises three main types: rock salt, solar salt, and evaporated salt. Rock salt is mined directly from underground salt deposits and is typically used for de-icing and industrial applications. Solar salt is produced through the evaporation of seawater or saltwater brine in shallow ponds, yielding natural minerals ideal for culinary and table use. Evaporated salt is created by boiling brine to extract pure salt crystals, often used in food processing and household applications for its fine texture and purity.</p></p>
