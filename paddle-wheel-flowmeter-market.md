<p><p>Paddle wheel flowmeters are essential for measuring fluid flow rates in various applications. The market features two primary types: tangential-flow and axis-flow paddle wheel flow meters. Tangential-flow paddle wheel flow meters measure flow through the interaction of fluid with the paddle positioned at an angle, enhancing accuracy in high-velocity applications. In contrast, axis-flow paddle wheel flow meters align the paddle parallel to the flow direction, providing reliable measurements across a wide range of fluid conditions and flow rates.</p></p>
