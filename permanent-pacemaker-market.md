<p><strong>The Permanent Pacemaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Dual-chamber Pacemaker</li><li>Three-chamber Pacemaker/Biventricular Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The permanent pacemaker market consists of three main types: Single-chamber pacemakers, which regulate heart rhythms by stimulating either the right atrium or ventricle; Dual-chamber pacemakers, which coordinate the contractions of both the atrium and ventricle for improved efficiency; and Three-chamber or biventricular pacemakers, designed to stimulate both ventricles, promoting synchronized heartbeats and enhancing cardiac output in heart failure patients. Each type addresses specific cardiac conditions and patient needs, driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
