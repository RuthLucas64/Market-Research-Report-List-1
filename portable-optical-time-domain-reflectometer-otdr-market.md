<p><p>The Portable Optical Time Domain Reflectometer (OTDR) is a specialized instrument used primarily in fiber optic networks to analyze and assess the integrity of optical fibers. It enables technicians to measure the light reflected from various points along the fiber and identify issues such as breaks, bends, or connectors that may result in signal loss. Due to its portability, it is particularly favored in field applications, offering versatility for installation, maintenance, and troubleshooting in telecommunications networks.</p><p>The Portable Optical Time Domain Reflectometer OTDR Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.70% during the forecast period.
