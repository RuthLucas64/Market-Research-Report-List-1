<p><strong>The Prefilled Disposable Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Simple Chamber</li><li>Dual Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prefilled disposable systems are categorized into two main types: simple chamber and dual chamber systems. Simple chamber systems contain a single medication compartment, allowing for easy and quick administration. They are ideal for straightforward dosing needs. In contrast, dual chamber systems feature two separate compartments that can mix two incompatible substances just before use, enhancing stability and efficacy. This distinction caters to varying patient requirements and drug properties, making both types essential in modern healthcare delivery.</p></p>
