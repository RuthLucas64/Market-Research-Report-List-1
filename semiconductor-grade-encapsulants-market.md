<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor grade encapsulants market is segmented into one-component and two-component types. One-component encapsulants are pre-mixed formulations that simplify the application process, making them ideal for automated assembly. They cure through exposure to heat or moisture, offering convenience and consistency. In contrast, two-component encapsulants require mixing before application, allowing for tailored properties and enhanced performance characteristics. This flexibility caters to specific application demands, providing manufacturers with options for optimal thermal, electrical, and mechanical protective solutions in semiconductor packaging.</p></p>
