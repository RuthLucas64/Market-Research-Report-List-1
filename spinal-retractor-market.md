<p><p>The spinal retractor market is divided into two primary types: tubular retractors and blade retractors. Tubular retractors are designed for minimally invasive surgeries, allowing surgeons to access the spine through smaller incisions while minimizing tissue damage. In contrast, blade retractors are used in open surgeries, providing broader exposure of the surgical site. Both types are essential tools for spinal procedures, enhancing visibility and access for surgeons, ultimately contributing to better patient outcomes and recovery times.</p></p>
