<p><p>The steam cleaning machine market is divided into three main types: high pressure-type, multifunctional-type, and superheated-type. High pressure-type machines deliver intense steam under pressure, effectively removing tough stains and grease. Multifunctional-type machines offer versatile cleaning options, often combining steam with other cleaning features for various surfaces. Superheated-type machines heat water beyond boiling, creating dry steam that penetrates surfaces deeply, killing bacteria and allergens while minimizing moisture. Each type addresses specific cleaning needs in commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
