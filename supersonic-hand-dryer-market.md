<p><p>The supersonic hand dryer market comprises primarily two types: hot air hand dryers and jet air hand dryers. Hot air hand dryers utilize warm air to evaporate moisture from hands, providing a more traditional drying method that is energy-efficient but slower. In contrast, jet air hand dryers employ high-speed jets of air to rapidly remove water, offering a quicker and often more hygienic solution. Both types serve the need for efficient hand drying in commercial and public restrooms.</p></p>
