<p><strong>The Telecentric Camera Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Object Square Telephoto Lens</li><li>Bi-Telecentric Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The telecentric camera lens market includes distinct types such as object square telephoto lenses and bi-telecentric lenses. Object square telephoto lenses maintain consistent magnification across the field, reducing perspective distortion, making them ideal for industrial applications where accurate measurements are crucial. Bi-telecentric lenses, on the other hand, feature telecentricity in both object and image space, ensuring parallel light rays, which enhances measurement precision and minimizes optical errors. Both lens types are vital in automation, machine vision, and quality control applications.</p></p>
