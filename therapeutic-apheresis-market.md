<p><strong>The Therapeutic Apheresis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Therapeutic Plasma Exchange</li><li>Red Blood Cell Exchange</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The therapeutic apheresis market is primarily categorized into Therapeutic Plasma Exchange (TPE) and Red Blood Cell Exchange (RBCE). TPE involves the removal of plasma from the blood to treat conditions like autoimmune diseases and certain neurological disorders, facilitating the replacement of harmful substances with donor plasma or replacement fluids. RBCE focuses on the extraction and replacement of red blood cells, commonly used for sickle cell disease management and other hematologic conditions, improving oxygen delivery and reducing complications.</p></p>
