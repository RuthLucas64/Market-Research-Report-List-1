<p><p>Thermoplastic elastomer (TPE) flexible tubing comes in various types, including double tube and multi-tube configurations. The double tube type features two concentric tubes, providing a balance of flexibility and strength, ideal for applications requiring fluid or gas transport. Multi-tube type tubing consists of multiple channels within a single structure, allowing for simultaneous transport of different substances. Both types leverage TPE's unique properties, offering durability, chemical resistance, and ease of processing, making them suitable for diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
