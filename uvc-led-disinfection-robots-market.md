<p><p>The UVC LED disinfection robot market is primarily divided into two types: Ultraviolet Mercury Lamp Disinfection and UVC-LED Disinfection. Ultraviolet Mercury Lamp Disinfection utilizes traditional mercury vapor lamps, producing intense UV-C light for effective germicidal action but often has limitations in size, efficiency, and environmental impact. In contrast, UVC-LED Disinfection employs advanced LED technology, offering more energy efficiency, longer lifespan, and lower heat emission. These distinctions influence their applications in healthcare, public spaces, and various industries focusing on sanitation.</p></p>
