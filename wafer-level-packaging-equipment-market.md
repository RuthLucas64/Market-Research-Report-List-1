<p><p>The wafer-level packaging (WLP) equipment market encompasses various types, including Fan-In, Fan-Out, and Others. Fan-In packaging arranges multiple I/O pads closely on a single chip, ideal for high-density applications. Fan-Out packaging, on the other hand, allows for a larger number of I/Os by expanding the footprint beyond the chip, improving performance and thermal management. Other methods may include advanced techniques like 3D packaging or system-in-package (SiP), catering to diverse semiconductor needs and design complexities.</p></p>
