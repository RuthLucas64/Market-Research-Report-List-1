<p><ul><li>Fumed White Carbon Black</li><li>Precipitation White Carbon Black</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The market for white carbon blacks is divided primarily into two types: fumed white carbon black and precipitation white carbon black. Fumed white carbon black is produced through gas-phase oxidation, resulting in a fine, highly porous structure, often used in applications requiring high reinforcement levels, such as in silicones and specialty rubber. Precipitated white carbon black, on the other hand, is manufactured through chemical precipitation, offering a coarser texture suitable for various applications, including fillers in plastics and coatings.</p></p>
